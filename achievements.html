SMART INDIA HACKATHON - 2022

Hackathon Venue: Parul University, Vadodara, Gujarat

Event Date: 25 & 26 August'22

  • I advanced to the finals of the Smart India Hackathon,where I served as the team leader alongside my teammates and mentor.
  • My team, United Coderz, comprised five dedicated members.Our project focused on raising awareness about narcotics using augmented reality technology, and it was designed to address the critical issue of drug usage in society.
  • The competition consisted of a total of three challenging rounds, providing us with an excellent platform to engage with influential figures from various industries. The event was expertly organized, leaving a lasting impression on all participants.
  • We were proud to receive a participation award, and the recognition was doubly special because we received it first in Gujarat during the competition and then in our college. The awards were presented to us by our college principal and the Head of the Department
  • The entire experience allowed us to connect with influential individuals from different industries, making it a memorable and rewarding journey.
